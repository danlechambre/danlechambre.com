I think far too much about how we tend to define ourselves by our jobs. This can be stifling to our sense of self. We also have a tendency to expect the same from others, which explains what happens at almost every party or gathering we might attend. It normally plays out something like this:

:::screenplay

::transition[Fade in:]

::heading[Int. A Middle-class Millenial's House - Kitchen (probably) - Night]

::action[Strangers loosely affiliated to one another via the host mill around trying not to look as awkward as they feel, looking for (or perhaps avoiding) someone to talk to. BOB bumps into ALICE at the fridge, whilst they are both getting a drink.]

::character[Bob]

::dialogue[So what do you do, Alice?]

::character[Alice]

::paren[(sighs internally)]

::dialogue[I'm a frontend engineer. I build UI, usually SaaS apps for big companies.]

::action[Bob stares blankly at Alice, not quite sure what she said, and even less sure how to respond]

::character[ALICE]

::paren[(shuffling awkwardly)]

::dialogue[I make websites]

::character[BOB]

::dialogue[Oh cool! I've been having problems with my internet. It cuts out all of the time, I bet you'd be able to fix that right?]

::character[ALICE]

::dialogue[Hehe! Well that's not... yeah... probably]

::action[ALICE take a few sips with an awkward pause between each]

::character[ALICE]

::dialogue[What about you?]

:::

I'm sure we've all been there. And we all know just how painfully awkward it can be to keep one of those exchanges going. But what if we change it up, even a little?

:::screenplay

::transition[Scene rewinds to start:]

::heading[Int. A Middle-class Millenial's House - Kitchen (probably) - Night]

::action[Strangers loosely affiliated to one another via the host mill around trying not to look as awkward as they feel, looking for (or perhaps avoiding) someone to talk to. BOB bumps into ALICE at the fridge, whilst they are both getting a drink.]

::character[Bob]

::dialogue[So, Alice, how do you pass time when you're not working or doing life admin or whatever?]

::character[Alice]

::paren[(slightly startled, taken a aback)]

::dialogue[Gee, I, erm, I'm big in to climbing actually]

::character[BOB]

::paren[(eyes wide, smiling)]

::dialogue[Oh cool! Like, mountains and stuff?]

::character[ALICE]

::dialogue[Ha! Not quite, I'd love to do that someday though. I mostly just stay around Yorkshire]

::action[ALICE puts her drink down on the side]

::character[ALICE]

::dialogue[How about you?]
:::
