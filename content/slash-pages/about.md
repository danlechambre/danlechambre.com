A creative technologist based in the North of England. Right now, building for the web keeps me curious and keeps me paid. But I wouldn't say what I do for work _really_ captures what I'm about or what gets me out of bed in a morning.

So the admittedly wanky term of "creative technologist" is a way for me to say:

> I'm passionate about the creative arts, the magic of technology, and exploring ways to bring the two together in my own unique way.

I approach life and work with a polymathic spirit, prioritising exploration and curiosity over mastery. It just so happens that all of this aligns very well with being a successful web developer (dude's gotta eat right?).

If I could be free of the shackles of capitalism and could do _anything at all_ I'd probably be building games. I try to convince myself this is possible [now and again](/game-dev).
